About Our Foundation

আমাদের ফাউন্ডেশন সম্পর্কে

The Abdul Halim Khan Foundation has been at the forefront of healthcare service delivery in Bangladesh since 2000. Founded with a vision to ensure no one is denied medical care due to poverty, we have grown into a trusted healthcare organization serving hundreds of thousands of people.

আমরা বিশ্বাস করি প্রতিটি মানুষের মানসম্মত স্বাস্থ্যসেবা পাওয়ার অধিকার রয়েছে। আমাদের দক্ষ চিকিৎসক ও স্বেচ্ছাসেবকদের টিম দিনরাত কাজ করে যাচ্ছে এই লক্ষ্যে।

Our dedicated team of doctors, nurses, and volunteers work tirelessly across rural and urban Bangladesh to deliver primary care, maternal health, emergency response, and community health education.

AHK General Hospital

এ.এইচ.কে. জেনারেল হাসপাতাল

A 100-bed general hospital providing comprehensive inpatient and outpatient care including surgery, medicine, pediatrics, and emergency services to all patients regardless of ability to pay.

Maternal & Child Health

মাতৃ ও শিশু স্বাস্থ্য কার্যক্রম

Dedicated maternal and child healthcare units providing antenatal care, safe delivery services, postnatal support, and child immunization across rural Bangladesh.

Learn More

Mobile Health Teams

মোবাইল স্বাস্থ্যসেবা দল

Our mobile health teams travel to the most remote areas of Bangladesh, bringing essential medical care, medicines, and health education directly to underserved communities.

Learn More
🧠

Mental Health Program

মানসিক স্বাস্থ্য কার্যক্রম

Breaking the stigma around mental health in Bangladesh by providing counseling, community support groups, and psychiatric care to those in need across all districts.

Learn More
🦷

Dental Health Initiative

দন্ত স্বাস্থ্য উদ্যোগ

Free dental camps and permanent dental clinics providing oral health education, treatment, and surgery to children and adults who would otherwise have no access to dental care.

Disaster Response Unit
